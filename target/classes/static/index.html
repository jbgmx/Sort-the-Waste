    How to play

    • Drag an item into a bin — or use keyboard: Tab to focus an item, Enter to pick up, Tab to focus a bin, Enter to drop.
    • Each correct sort = +10 points. Wrong sort = lose a life and see why it’s wrong.
    • Finish 10 items before the timer runs out to beat the level!
    • Bin colours: Red = Landfill, Yellow = Recycling, Green = FOGO, Purple = Glass-only.

    Made for kids in Australia. Always check your local council rules — they can vary!
